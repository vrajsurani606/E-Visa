To Download a Graphic

PC- Click and hold the right mouse button on the map for 3 seconds, a window will pop up. Select Save Target As from the pop-up window and save the map to your desktop or to a folder.

Mac-Hold down the Control Key on your Keyboard and click on the Map with your pointer. A window will pop-up and you can either Copy Image or Save Image As the map to your computer.
